# Quote & Pricing Checklist

## Purpose
Standardised quote-building framework for all Zeus-Renovation client proposals. Ensures scope clarity, margin discipline, and professional presentation across HDB and condo projects.

---

## Quote Template Structure

### Section 1: Header
```
[[YOUR_COMPANY] Logo]
Quotation No: HFH-YYYY-MM-NNN
Date: [DD/MM/YYYY]
Valid Until: [DD/MM/YYYY] (30 days standard)
Client: [Full Name]
Property: [Address + Unit Type — HDB 4RM Resale / Condo 3BR / etc.]
Contact: [Phone / Email]
```

### Section 2: Scope Summary
One-paragraph narrative summarising the project scope. Example:
> "Full renovation of 4-room HDB resale flat at [address], including: full hacking of existing finishes, rewiring, bathroom waterproofing & tiling (2 bathrooms), built-in carpentry for kitchen, master bedroom wardrobe, and living room feature wall, plus painting throughout."

### Section 3: Trade Breakdown (Core)
Each trade as a separate line-item block with unit rate transparency:

| Trade | Scope Detail | Qty | Unit | Unit Rate | Total |
|-------|-------------|-----|------|-----------|-------|
| Demolition | Hacking of floor/wall tiles, carpentry removal | 1 | Lot | — | $X,XXX |
| Masonry | Bathroom wall/floor tiling (supply & lay) | 85 | sqm | $XX | $X,XXX |
| Carpentry | Kitchen cabinets (plywood carcass, laminate finish) | 32 | ft | $XXX | $X,XXX |

### Section 4: Exclusions & Clarifications (Mandatory)
- Electrical rewiring (by licensed electrician, separate quote)
- Air-con installation (by Gain City / authorised dealer)
- Appliances supply (client direct purchase)
- Window/grille replacement (requires HDB window contractor)
- Curtains, blinds, soft furnishings
- Post-renovation cleaning (chemical wash included; general cleaning excluded unless stated)

### Section 5: Payment Schedule
| Milestone | Percentage | Trigger |
|-----------|-----------|---------|
| Deposit | 10% | Upon acceptance of quotation |
| Phase 1 | 30% | Completion of demolition & MEP rough-in |
| Phase 2 | 30% | Completion of wet works & carpentry installation |
| Phase 3 | 25% | Completion of all finishing works |
| Retention | 5% | After defect rectification (30 days post-handover) |

### Section 6: Terms & Conditions
- Quotation validity: 30 days
- Variation orders (VO): confirmed in writing, priced at quoted unit rates where applicable
- Defect liability period: 12 months on workmanship
- Warranty: manufacturer warranty on materials; 12 months workmanship warranty
- Client responsibilities: vacant possession during works, access for inspections

## Pricing Tiers

### Tier 1: Budget (HDB BTO / Basic Overlay)
- **Target margin**: 15–18%
- **Tile range**: $3.50–$5.50 psf
- **Carpentry**: Melamine carcass, laminate finish
- **Typical project**: $25K–$40K
- **Client profile**: Newlyweds, first-time homeowners, tight budget

### Tier 2: Mid-Range (HDB Resale Full Reno / Condo Overlay)
- **Target margin**: 18–25%
- **Tile range**: $5.50–$12.00 psf
- **Carpentry**: Plywood carcass, textured laminate / veneer finish
- **Typical project**: $45K–$80K
- **Client profile**: Upgraders, young families, quality-conscious

### Tier 3: Premium (Landed / Penthouse / Full Condo Gut)
- **Target margin**: 22–30%
- **Tile range**: $12.00–$35.00+ psf (sintered stone slabs, large-format tiles)
- **Carpentry**: Solid plywood, veneer, fluted panels, integrated LED
- **Typical project**: $80K–$200K+
- **Client profile**: Affluent, design-driven, willing to pay for detail

## Margin Calculation Template
```
Client Price  = (Supply Cost + Labour Cost + Overhead Allocation) × (1 + Margin %)
Overhead Allocation: ~8–12% of direct costs (transport, tools, consumables, supervision, warranty reserve)
```
### Margin Check Gates
- [ ] **Gate 1**: Per-trade margin ≥ 12% (flag if negative on any trade)
- [ ] **Gate 2**: Blended project margin ≥ 18% (Tier 1), ≥ 20% (Tier 2), ≥ 22% (Tier 3)
- [ ] **Gate 3**: Exclusions clearly listed — no scope creep exposure
- [ ] **Gate 4**: Payment schedule aligned to cash outlay — subcontractors paid after client milestone

## Client Presentation Format

### Option A: WhatsApp Quick Send
```
[Project Name] — Renovation Quote Summary
✅ Scope: [One-liner]
💰 Total: $XX,XXX
📋 Includes: [Bullet list of key items]
❌ Excludes: [Bullet list]
📅 Timeline: [Weeks]
Full quote PDF emailed to [email]. Let me know if you'd like to walk through it!
```

### Option B: Detailed Email
1. Personalised opening referencing site visit or previous conversation
2. Embedded PDF of detailed quote
3. Key highlights (3–5 bullet points of what makes this quote competitive)
4. Clear next step CTA: "Shall we schedule a 30-min call to go through this?"
5. Signature: [NAME_REDACTED], [YOUR_COMPANY], [phone], [email]

### Option C: In-Person Pitch Deck (iPad/Procreate)
- Slide 1: Mood board (Japandi / Modern Luxe / etc.)
- Slide 2: Floor plan with overlay annotations
- Slide 3: Scope highlights with before/after concepts
- Slide 4: Quote summary with trade breakdown
- Slide 5: Timeline visual
- Slide 6: Why [YOUR_COMPANY] (USP: trusted advisor, transparent pricing, quality workmanship)
