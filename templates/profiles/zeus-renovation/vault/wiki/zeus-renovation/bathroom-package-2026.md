# Bathroom-Only Renovation Package
# Created: June 2026
# Property: Typical HDB Bathroom (Master or Common)
# Assumptions: Resale flat (full hacking required), ~100 sqft floor area, standard ceiling height
# Target Margin: 18-25% blended across all tiers

---

## STRATEGY

**Why this package works:**
- Lowers barrier to entry — client says "yes" to bathroom, then trusts you with full reno later
- Higher margin concentration — bathroom is labour-heavy with concentrated scope
- Repeat referral machine — bathroom is the most visible room for neighbours/family
- Pipeline builder — clients who start with bathroom often come back for full renovation within 6-12 months

**Positioning:** All-in, no-surprise pricing. Every competitor gap we identified (hidden VOs, exclusion creep) gets closed here.

| Band | Typical Unit | Floor Area | Wall Area | Application |

Pricing below is for Standard (master) bathroom. Small bathroom deduct ~15-20%.

## BTO-BATHROOM VARIANT

For BTO units, adjust as follows:
- **Demolition cost REDUCED** — no existing tiles to hack typically, HDB basic finishes may remain
- **HDB optional works** — if they already took HDB flooring package, confirm if floor tile needs replacement or overlay
- **Price adjustment:** Deduct ~$400-600 demolition savings from each tier
- **Timeline:** 8-10 working days (no hacking phase)

## PACKAGING & PRICING RECOMMENDATIONS

1. **Anchor on Standard tier.** Present Standard first as "what most clients choose." Essential is for budget-conscious, Premium is for aspiration. Behavioral pricing 101.

2. **Show a sample photo set per tier.** Even rough mood boards in Procreate help clients visualise the difference between "basic ceramic" and "large-format porcelain."

3. **Use bathroom jobs as trust builders.** A well-executed bathroom at fair price earns referrals faster than any marketing. Word-of-mouth in HDB estates is the strongest lead source.

4. **Track upsell conversion.** How many bathroom clients come back for full renovation? Target: 30-40% upsell within 12 months.

5. **Standardise site photos.** Document every bathroom project with before/during/after. This becomes your portfolio and social proof simultaneously.
