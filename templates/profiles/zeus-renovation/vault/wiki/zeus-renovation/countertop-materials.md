# Singapore Countertop Materials Reference

## Material Types

| Material | Common Thickness | Type | Key Properties |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Sintered Stone** | 12mm / 15mm | Engineered | High heat resistance, UV stable, large format |
| **Quartz Surface** | 20mm | Engineered | Non-porous, durable, wide colour range |
| **Solid Surface** | — | Acrylic/Polyester | Seamless joins, repairable, thermoformable |
| **Granite** | — | Natural Stone | Heat-proof, scratch-resistant, requires sealing |
| **Marble** | — | Natural Stone | Elegant veining, porous, needs maintenance |
| **Terrazzo** | — | Composite | Cement or resin base with marble/glass chips |
| **Volcanic / Lava Stone** | — | Natural Stone | Textured finish, heat-resistant, unique look |
