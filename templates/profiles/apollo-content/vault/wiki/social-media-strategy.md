# Social Media Strategy — Apollo Content

## Platform Matrix

| Platform | Role | Post Frequency | Content Formats |
|---|---|---|---|
| **LinkedIn** | Thought leadership, B2B trust | 4x/week | Long-form text posts, carousels, polls |
| **Instagram** | Brand aesthetic, reel discovery | 5x/week | Reels, carousels, Stories |
| **X (Twitter)** | Rapid takes, community conversation | 2-3x/day | Threads, quotes, replies |
| **YouTube** | Long-form education, SEO asset | 2x/month | 8-15 min tutorials, case studies |
| **TikTok** | Top-of-funnel, trend participation | 3x/week | Short-form (15-60s), duets, stitches |
| **Email** | Owned audience, direct conversion | 1x/week | Curated newsletter, deep dives |

**Priority order:** LinkedIn > Instagram > Email > YouTube > X > TikTok. We'd rather win on three platforms than be mediocre on six.

## Content Pillars

Every post maps to exactly one pillar. If it doesn't fit, it doesn't ship.

1. **Educate** (40% of output) — How-tos, frameworks, industry analysis, myth-busting. The "save this" content.
2. **Inspire** (25%) — Case studies, behind-the-scenes, founder stories. The "I want that" content.
3. **Entertain** (15%) — Memes, hot takes, relatable industry humour. The "tag a colleague" content.
4. **Sell** (10%) — Product launches, offers, testimonials. The "buy now" content.
5. **Engage** (10%) — Polls, questions, UGC reposts, community spotlights. The "join the conversation" content.

## Hashtag Strategy

**Rule of 3-5:** Never use more than 5 hashtags per post. More reads as desperate.

**Tiered approach:**
- **Tier 1 — Branded (always):** e.g., `#BuiltWithApollo`, `#ApolloContent`
- **Tier 2 — Niche (2-3 per post):** e.g., `#ContentStrategy`, `#BrandBuilding`, `#SoloPreneur`
- **Tier 3 — Reach (1-2 per post):** e.g., `#Marketing`, `#BusinessGrowth`

Rotate Tier 2 and Tier 3 tags weekly to avoid shadowban patterns. Track which tags drive profile visits in native analytics — kill underperformers monthly.

## Engagement Metrics That Matter

**Vanity metrics to ignore:** follower count, raw likes, vanity impressions.

**Action metrics to track (weekly):**
1. **Save rate** (saves ÷ impressions) — strongest signal of educational quality
2. **Share rate** (shares ÷ impressions) — strongest signal of emotional resonance
3. **Comment depth** (average comment length, replies per thread) — signal of community health
4. **Link clicks** (especially on LinkedIn and email) — signal of conversion intent
5. **Reply time** (median minutes to first response on comments) — signal of community responsiveness

**Target benchmarks:**
- LinkedIn save rate: >4%
- Instagram reel completion rate: >60%
- Email open rate: >35%
- Comment reply time: <2 hours during business hours

## Quarterly Review

Every quarter, audit all platforms:
- Kill channels with <10% audience overlap and <2% conversion contribution.
- Double down on the top-performing content pillar — shift 5% budget from the weakest.
- Refresh hashtag tiers based on the last 90 days of data.
- Archive underperforming posts (likes <5% of account average) after 7 days.
