# Hermes Blueprint — Product Positioning

## Core Positioning

**Hermes Blueprint turns messy business workflows into AI-assisted systems in 30 days. It includes a guided setup path for both technical and non-technical users, a 48-hour First Business Win wizard, and a 30-day guided personalization system that turns Hermes into a business-specific AI operating partner.**

The differentiator is not only the profiles — it's the activation path after installation:

> Choose setup path → Install → Personalization → Pick first business win → 48-hour first artifact → Review Week 1 → Identify safe automations → Measure 30-day ROI

## Primary Promise

**Choose your setup path. Install Hermes. Create your first usable business artifact within 48 hours. Then spend the next 30 days teaching Hermes your business until it becomes useful for your actual work.**

## One-Line Pitch

A guided AI workflow system with 13 specialist agents, 3 setup paths (DIY / Guided / Done-With-You), a 48-hour First Business Win wizard, and a 30-day personalization path — so Hermes doesn't just install, it helps create one usable business artifact first, then keeps learning your business.

## Short Website Pitch

Most AI tools stop after setup. Hermes Blueprint gives you 3 setup paths to match your technical confidence, the installer, 13 specialist AI profiles, a First Business Win wizard to create one usable artifact within 48 hours, and a guided 30-day personalization path that helps Hermes learn your services, workflow, customers, tone, approval boundaries, and first high-value use cases.

## What Makes It Different

| Generic AI Tool | Hermes Blueprint |
|---|---|
| One chatbot | 13 specialist profiles |
| One-size-fits-all setup | DIY, Guided, or Done-With-You path based on confidence |
| Generic prompts | Customer context captured during onboarding + First Business Win wizard |

## Emotional Value Proposition

Customers should feel:

- **Guided** — "I know what to do next."
- **Understood** — "It asked about my business, not a generic user."
- **In control** — "It knows what needs approval."
- **Progressive** — "It gets more useful each week."
- **Worth it** — "I paid for a system and path, not just files."

## Recommended Product Language

Use:

- "Guided setup path" (DIY / Guided / Done-With-You)
- "First Business Win"
- "48-hour first artifact"
- "Guided personalization path"
- "30-day success path"
- "Teach Hermes your business"
- "AI workflow system"
- "Specialist profiles"
- "Approval boundaries"
- "Week 1 review"
- "30-day ROI review"

Avoid:

- "Just install and start asking"
- "No onboarding needed"
- "That's it"
- "Generic AI assistant"
- "Chatbot"
- "AI business OS" (too abstract for non-technical buyers — use "AI workflow system" instead)
