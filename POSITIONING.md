# Hermes Blueprint — Product Positioning

## Core Positioning

**Hermes Blueprint is not just a 13-profile AI installer. It is a 30-day guided personalization system that turns Hermes into a business-specific AI operating partner.**

The differentiator is the path after installation:

> Install → Teach Hermes your business → Build first workflow → Review Week 1 → Identify safe automations → Measure 30-day ROI

## Primary Promise

**Install your AI business OS today. Spend the next 30 days teaching it your business until it becomes useful for your actual work.**

## One-Line Pitch

A self-hosted AI business OS with 13 specialist agents and a built-in 30-day personalization path — so it doesn't just install, it learns your business.

## Short Website Pitch

Most AI tools stop after setup. Hermes Blueprint gives you the installer, 13 specialist AI profiles, and a guided 30-day personalization path that helps your Hermes learn your services, workflow, customers, tone, approval boundaries, and first high-value use cases.

## What Makes It Different

| Generic AI Tool | Hermes Blueprint |
|---|---|
| One chatbot | 13 specialist profiles |
| Generic prompts | Customer context captured during onboarding |
| SaaS subscription | Self-hosted, one-time purchase |
| User must figure out what to ask | Built-in first prompts and 30-day success path |
| No memory structure | Profile vaults and customer context files |
| Setup ends at install | Setup continues through Day 0, Day 7, Day 30 |

## Emotional Value Proposition

Customers should feel:

- **Guided** — "I know what to do next."
- **Understood** — "It asked about my business, not a generic user."
- **In control** — "It knows what needs approval."
- **Progressive** — "It gets more useful each week."
- **Worth it** — "I paid for a system and path, not just files."

## Buyer Objections and Counters

| Objection | Counter |
|---|---|
| "I don't know what to ask AI." | Blueprint creates first prompts and a 30-day success path. |
| "It sounds too technical." | Pro tier includes Day 0 guided setup and Day 7 review. |
| "Will it understand my business?" | The personalization interview captures your services, workflow, tools, tone, and boundaries. |
| "What happens after install?" | Day 1 workflow interview, Day 3 playbook, Day 7 review, Day 14 automation audit, Day 30 ROI review. |
| "Is this just ChatGPT with folders?" | No — it is multi-profile routing, vault context, scripts, backups, health checks, and guided personalization. |
