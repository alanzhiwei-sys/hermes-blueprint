# Hermes Blueprint — Pricing Model

**Currency:** Singapore Dollars (SGD)  
**Model:** One-time purchase, no recurring fees  
**Customer also pays:** LLM API costs (~$25-50 SGD/mo via OpenRouter/Anthropic)

---

| Tier | Price (SGD) | What's Included | Best For |
|---|---|---|---|

| **Blueprint Foundry** | **$999** | Everything in Pro + Alan personally customizes 3 profiles for their specific industry + 3 one-hour strategy sessions (within 90 days) + priority WhatsApp support for 90 days. | Businesses who want a tailored AI OS, not off-the-shelf |

The PDF alone is $79; for $70 more they get everything Alan built. Self-selects for technical buyers who won't need hand-holding. |

## What's NOT Included (Be Honest)

- ❌ Linux system administration (customer needs basic Linux knowledge or a tech-savvy friend)
- ❌ LLM API credits (customer pays OpenRouter/Anthropic directly)
- ❌ Custom software development (this is a pre-built Blueprint, not a dev agency)
- ❌ Guaranteed uptime or SLA (this runs on the customer's hardware)
- ❌ Phone support (email/WhatsApp only, during Singapore business hours)
- ❌ Ongoing profile updates beyond 90 days (Foundry tier only)
