# Hermes Blueprint — Pricing Model

**Currency:** Singapore Dollars (SGD)  
**Model:** One-time purchase, no recurring fees  
**Customer also pays:** LLM API costs (~$25-50 SGD/mo via OpenRouter/Anthropic)

---

## Positioning

Hermes Blueprint is priced as a **guided AI business OS**, not just a folder of profiles.

The value stack is:

1. **Installer** — gets Hermes running
2. **Setup path** — DIY, Guided, or Done-With-You based on confidence level
3. **13 specialist profiles** — gives the customer an AI team
4. **Personalization interview** — teaches Hermes the customer's business
5. **First Business Win** — creates one usable artifact within 48 hours
6. **30-day success path** — prevents the "that's it?" feeling after installation
7. **Review loops** — Day 7 and Day 30 make value visible

## Value Framing

Do **not** sell it as:

> "13 AI profiles and an installer."

Sell it as:

> "A guided AI workflow system that helps a small business turn one messy workflow into a usable AI-assisted system within 48 hours — then personalizes over 30 days."

> "A self-hosted AI business OS with 13 specialist profiles and a 30-day guided path to personalize it to your business."

## What's NOT Included — Be Honest

- ❌ Linux system administration beyond documented supported paths
- ❌ LLM API credits — customer pays OpenRouter/Anthropic directly
- ❌ Custom software development — this is a pre-built Blueprint, not a dev agency
- ❌ Guaranteed uptime or SLA — this runs on the customer's hardware
- ❌ Customer-message auto-sending without explicit approval
- ❌ Ongoing profile updates beyond the stated support window

---

## Refund Policy

- **PDF:** No refunds — digital product, instant delivery
- **Kit:** 7-day refund if installation fails on supported hardware after following the documented path
- **Pro / Foundry:** Full refund minus $100 if cancelled before the first call; no refund after the first call
