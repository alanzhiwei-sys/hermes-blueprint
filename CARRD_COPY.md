## SECTION 8: FAQ

**Q: "What happens after installation?"**
A: Hermes runs a personalization interview, creates your customer context, and gives you a Day 0 / Day 1 / Day 7 / Day 14 / Day 30 path. You are not left staring at a blank prompt box.

**Q: "I'm not technical. Can I use this?"**
A: Yes, but choose Guided Setup. DIY needs terminal comfort. Guided Setup exists so business owners can focus on the workflow problem, not the Linux/API details.

**Q: "Will it understand my business?"**
A: Not magically on Day 0. The personalization path teaches Hermes your services, ideal customers, workflow, tools, tone, and approval boundaries. It gets better as you add real context.

**Q: "What's the total cost?"**
A: $149 one-time for the Kit. Plus $25-50 SGD/month for AI usage, paid directly to OpenRouter/Anthropic. No SaaS subscription to us.

**Q: "Is my data safe?"**
A: Everything runs on YOUR machine. Client data, project files, emails — nothing goes to us. The only external call is to your chosen AI provider for text generation.

**Q: "Can I try before buying?"**
A: The open-source core is free on GitHub. Install it, use it, and decide later if you want the commercial Kit, guided onboarding, or custom profiles.

**Q: "Who's behind this?"**
A: Alan — a renovation project manager in Singapore. Built this for himself, used it daily, and now sharing it with other operators who want practical AI instead of hype.
